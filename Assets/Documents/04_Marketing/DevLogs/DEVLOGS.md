## Recording & Editing Best Practices
- Video: 1920x1080 (60fps if gameplay looks smooth), use a capture bitrate of 10â€“20 Mbps for clear footage.
- Audio: use a USB/XLR microphone with pop filter. Record audio at 44.1/48 kHz, 16â€“24 bit. Normalize and apply light compression & noise gate.
- Webcam: optional, 720p minimum, place a small rounded webcam overlay.
- Screen recording: record game 1080p; if possible, record editor windows separately for cutaways.
- Editing software: DaVinci Resolve (free), Premiere Pro, or CapCut for mobile edits.
- Editing checklist: trim dead air, add captions, add lower-thirds with episode number, include music (royalty-free), color grade gameplay for consistency, export H.264 MP4.
