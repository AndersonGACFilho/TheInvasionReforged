# Early Access Strategy

**Project:** The Invasion: Reforged  
**Launch:** Week 14 (Feb 16, 2026)  
**Duration:** 3-4 months  
**Full Release:** Week 25+ (Apr 28, 2026)

---

## Rationale

**Early Access Benefits:**
- Launch 5 weeks earlier (Week 14 vs Week 19)
- Revenue funds post-launch development
- Player feedback shapes content updates
- Multiple marketing moments (EA + 3 updates + full release)
- Lower pressure ("in development" vs "complete")

**Traditional Launch Risks:**
- 19 weeks cash flow delay
- Design locked without player validation
- Single launch window
- Must ship "complete" (5 arenas, 3-phase boss)

---

**Weeks 1-6:** Art production (all assets)

## Risk Mitigation

**Technical Risks:**
- Feature freeze Week 11 prevents scope creep
- Week 11 playtesting validates fun factor
- Mobile build tested early

**Market Risks:**
- Multiple marketing moments reduce launch pressure
- EA pricing lower ($7.99) vs full ($9.99)
- Premium mobile appeals to quality-focused audience

**Development Risks:**
- Art-first locks visual scope
- Component architecture enables isolated testing
- 90%+ test coverage reduces regression bugs
